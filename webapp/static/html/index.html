Welcome To My Plant Processor!

What is it?

After picking up my first plant right before moving into my college apartment, I was wondering if I would have a way to be able to remember when it needed to be watered. However, it really wasn't an issue for just ONE plant.

But now, my veritable army of four seems like it requires a lot more attention and bookkeeping when it comes to watering. Therefore, I decided to take it upon myself to make some sort of application to keep track of said waterings.

It just so happens that I'm currently enrolled in an internet programming course, and what better way to keep track of plants than with a fullstack web application? (That's what I tell myself at least) Thus, Peter's Plant Processor was born.

Functionality

Once this is actually finished, it will be a way for me to log in from anywhere and update information about my plants.

Not only to update information, but also see about when certain plants need to be watered and what kinds of conditions they should live in.
